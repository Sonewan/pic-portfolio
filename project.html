Hello,I am Shubhra.A photograph is an image that is recorded that the human eye can perceive. Photography is the way of capturing those valuable moments which can open the gateway to the past. They are intrinsic. The photograph remains the constant witness of a frozen movement. Photography is a way of feeling, of touching,
of loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever. It remembers little things with those minor details, long after you have forgotten everything. However, photography is all about the
heart and mind of the photographer. It's about the perception of art that he holds which creates the masterpiece. He can record images with his mind or on film. There is no difference between the two. And sometimes, in photography there is a
reality so subtle that it becomes more real than reality.

I at Click_Fix bring to you this amazing collection of photographs that are so vibrant that,
if you listen carefully, will sing to you the splendour and memories of the moments.
They can be your gateway to those magnificent mountains or to those picturesque gardens
without even striding! I try to portray the best photographs that keep you transfixed and gazed at the very beauty of mother nature. Thank you.
